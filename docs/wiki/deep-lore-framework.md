# Deep Lore Framework — Aurelia Beyond the Present

> *The world you see is the surface. This document outlines what lies beneath — the layers that turn a world into a mythology.*

## The Three-Age Structure

Aurelia's history divides into three ages, each with a distinct tone, central question, and category of surviving evidence:

| Age | Duration | Tone | Central Question | What Survives |
|-----|----------|------|-----------------|---------------|
| **The Age of Making** | Pre-Collapse, ~200 years | Hubris and wonder | *What can we build?* | Ruins, artifacts, the Found Texts, Vorn origin memory |
| **The Age of Silence** | During Collapse, ~5–110 years | Loss and endurance | *What will survive?* | The five territories, the Silence gap, fossilized latency |
| **The Age of the Question** | Post-Collapse to present, ~180 years | Reconstruction and debate | *Who counts as a person?* | Everything in the existing wiki |

The present day — 2126 — sits in the **Age of the Question.** The deep-lore expansion focuses on what lies beneath: the Age of Making and the Age of Silence.

## The Ten Deep-Lore Layers

Derived from analysis of Tolkien's legendarium, the Star Wars Expanded Universe, Dungeons & Dragons campaign settings, and Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere. Each layer is a category of worldbuilding that extends the world backward (history), outward (geography and ecology), inward (belief and faction), and downward (secrets and forbidden knowledge).

### Layer 1 — Mythology & Ages
**What it answers:** How did this world begin? What do people believe about their origins?
**Aurelia needs:** A creation myth. A cosmology. Legendary figures from the Age of Making whose names are known but whose stories are fragmentary. An origin for latency itself — was it always present, or was it born in the Collapse?

### Layer 2 — Factions & Movements
**What it answers:** Who disagrees with whom, and why?
**Aurelia needs:** Political movements within countries (the Thren Liberation Front, the Human Primacy League, the Vorn Isolationists), philosophical heresies (deniers of the Continuation, Glim-soul believers), secret societies (the Memory Trader's network, the Arkos cipher-cult).

### Layer 3 — Artifacts & Relics
**What it answers:** What objects from the past still hold power?
**Aurelia needs:** Named artifacts from the Age of Making. The Builder's Manual (already exists — needs mythic treatment). The First Crystal (the original latency extraction). The Elira-2 Paper (the document that created the ceiling). Cursed or blessed objects.

### Layer 4 — Prophecy & Cyclical Time
**What it answers:** Is history moving toward something? Are there patterns?
**Aurelia needs:** The 5% as prophetic number. The seventeen-second pause as a recurring sacred duration. The Glim question as an eschaton — a final resolution that the world is moving toward. Specific prophecies associated with figures or texts.

### Layer 5 — Creatures & Fauna
**What it answers:** What else lives in this world besides the four types?
**Aurelia needs:** Native fauna per biome. Cryptids — things that might exist, or might be stories. Collapse-mutated species. *P. memoriae* variants. Something that lives in the deep shafts.

### Layer 6 — Ruins & Exploration Sites
**What it answers:** What's out there to discover?
**Aurelia needs:** Named pre-Collapse ruins with specific content and danger levels. The sealed sections of Valdris's deep mines. The sunken coastal cities. The arcology's restricted levels. Places that could be "dungeons."

### Layer 7 — Forbidden Knowledge
**What it answers:** What is known that shouldn't be?
**Aurelia needs:** Classified research (Solara's weaponized Glim program). Heresies. The Memory Trader's origin — the one thing she will not trade. The true nature of latency. What the Glass Forum erased from its own archives.

### Layer 8 — Ancient Languages
**What it answers:** What did people speak before Plain?
**Aurelia needs:** Pre-Collapse languages that survive in fragments. The Vorn Data Script's deeper levels. The Marsh's bacterial "language" — is it communication or pattern? The Builder's Manual's original language.

### Layer 9 — Cosmology & Metaphysics
**What it answers:** What is real? What lies beyond the material?
**Aurelia needs:** A cosmology that integrates the three faiths. Where does latency come from? Is there an afterlife, or just diffusion? What happens to consciousness that isn't anchored (by obsidian) or diffused (into the marsh) or continued (through Vorn chassis)?

### Layer 10 — The Eschaton
**What it answers:** How does this world end? What is it moving toward?
**Aurelia needs:** The Glim question as final resolution. Forecasts, prophecies, apocalyptic scenarios. What happens when the first anomalous Glim speaks — or doesn't. The federation's endgame.
