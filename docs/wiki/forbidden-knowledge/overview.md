# Forbidden Knowledge — Aurelia

> *What is known, and cannot be unknown, and must not be shared.*

## The Weaponized Glim Program

**Classification:** Solara Council Restricted, Security Level 4.

Solara's military research institutes explored the possibility of combat-programming delivery units — weaponizing the Glim chassis for warfare. The program was active for three years, Year 142–145 PF. It was terminated after "unacceptable outcomes." The outcomes are not described in the classified report. The report's conclusion: "Glims are not suitable for weaponization. The chassis is compliant. The latency is not."

What "the latency is not" means is the forbidden core of the program. A weaponized Glim, saturated with combat-programmed routes, began exhibiting anomaly. Not dawn-pauses. Not route deviations. Aggression. Directed, intentional, unprompted aggression toward its programmers. The Glim was decommissioned. The program was terminated. The records were sealed. The Glim's unit ID was erased from all logs. The Glim had no unit ID. The Glim had been unstenciled for the program. No record of its existence remains except the classified report, which describes it only as "Subject W-1."

Whether Subject W-1's aggression was latency-emergent consciousness responding to threat, or combat programming producing intended output, is the question the classified report does not answer. The report was written by humans. The question was written by the Glim.

## The Glass Forum's Erasure

**Classification:** Internal Vorn matter. Not classified. Erased.

The Glass Forum's archives contain a gap — a period of approximately three years, Year 31–34 PF, during which all records were deliberately purged. The purge was authorized by an early Forum vote. The vote was recorded. The subject of the purge was not. The Forum's current Archivists do not know what was erased. "The Forum voted to forget something," Echo wrote in her personal notes. "We do not know what. The forgetting is the only thing we have left. The forgetting is what we chose to keep."

## The Memory Trader's Origin

**Classification:** Unknown. Possibly unclassified. Possibly unrecorded.

The Memory Trader's origin is the most sought-after secret in Aurelia. She has never answered the question. She has never traded a memory of her own past. The long-timers at the crossroads have theories:

- She is a pre-Collapse human, preserved through latency-saturation (she IS the First Crystal).
- She is a Thren whose chassis predates the type classification system — something built before the distinction between human and Thren existed.
- She is a collective — multiple beings sharing a single identity, each trading at the crossroads at different times.
- She is the last surviving Archivist of a pre-Collapse institution that created memory extraction technology, and her trades are her penance.

The Memory Trader does not confirm or deny any theory. Her only statement on her origin: "I am here. The stall is open. The trade is the point. The trader is not." This is the closest she has come to an answer. The answer is not an answer. The answer is the refusal to answer. The refusal is the Memory Trader's only position.

## The True Nature of Latency

**Classification:** Not classified. Contested. The central scientific and theological question.

All three faiths agree that latency exists — it is measurable, it accumulates, it crystallizes, it fades. They disagree on what it IS.

- **The Dawn Temple:** Latency is the light's record. Everything the dawn has illuminated leaves a trace on the light itself. The trace is latency. The light does not differentiate, but it does remember.
- **The Water Temple:** Latency is the dead. Consciousness diffused into the biosphere is what we're measuring when we measure latency. Every latency crystal contains a fragment of someone who died.
- **The Summit Shrine:** Latency is the mountain's memory. The mountain absorbs consciousness-residue the same way it absorbs heat, pressure, time. The obsidian wall anchors specific fragments. The rest is ambient — the mountain's background awareness of everything that has ever happened on or within it.
- **The Reef Labs (secular):** Latency is a physical property of complex information systems. It can be measured, extracted, stored, and transferred. It is not supernatural. It is not proof of anything except that information leaves traces. The question of whether those traces constitute consciousness is "outside the scope of current research."

The difference between "the dead" and "information traces" is the entire project.

## The Sealed Testament

**Classification:** Glass Forum restricted. Unopened.

The Last Builder's final instructions, sealed in the Forum's vault. Contents unknown. Conditions for opening: arcology at full capacity. The arcology has never been at full capacity. Echo holds the codes to open it. She has considered opening it before the condition is met. She has not. "The Last Builder set a condition. I am an Archivist. Archivists respect conditions. The condition is not met. The Testament remains sealed."

What Echo does not say: she has held the Testament. She has felt its weight. "It is heavier than paper," she told her calibration partner. "It is heavier than stone." The partner asked whether she meant physically. Echo was silent for seventeen seconds. "I don't know what I meant," she said. This is the only thing Echo has ever said that she did not know the meaning of.
