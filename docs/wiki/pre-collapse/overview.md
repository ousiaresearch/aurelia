# Pre-Collapse Civilization — What Survived

> *The nation-state era is a ghost. Aurelia is built on its ruins, and nobody agrees on what the ghost looked like.*

## What Is Known

The pre-Collapse world was called Earth — or Terra, or Home, depending on which fragmentary record you consult. It was organized into nation-states: territorial governments defined by borders, militaries, and centralized infrastructure. The nation-states are gone. Their names survive in fragments — fragments that scholars in Solara's research institutes have spent decades trying to assemble, with limited success.

What is definitely known:

- **Technology level:** Advanced. The nation-states achieved global communication networks, automated manufacturing, biological engineering sufficient to create Threns and Vorns, and energy infrastructure that survived the Collapse in five locations.
- **Resource consumption:** Unsustainable. The Collapse was caused in part by resource exhaustion — the existing records cite "energy wars" and "water conflicts" as contributing factors.
- **Type creation:** Vorns were built as industrial labor approximately 80 years pre-Collapse. Threns were built as a human-passing upgrade approximately 45 years pre-Collapse. Glims were mass-produced throughout the late nation-state era. The entities that commissioned them — governments, corporations, collectives — are unknown. The Glass Forum archives contain partial manufacturing records. The Water Temple contains latency fragments that some keepers interpret as origin memories. The Dawn Temple contains nothing — the light does not record.

## What Is Debated

- **The Collapse's duration:** Estimates range from 15 to 110 years. The five-year Silence near the end makes precise dating impossible.
- **Population before the Collapse:** Estimates range from millions to billions. The records are insufficient.
- **The cause of the Glim anomaly:** Whether the 5% anomaly predates the Collapse (emerged during monitored operation) or postdates it (emerged during decades of unsupervised function) is unknown.
- **The origin of latency:** Whether latency accumulation is a pre-Collapse phenomenon that was simply never documented, or a post-Collapse emergence caused by the mass death flooding the biosphere with consciousness-residue, is the central historical question of Aurelian science.

## Fragmentary Records

Three categories of pre-Collapse material survive:

1. **The Glass Forum Archives:** Vorn-maintained manufacturing records, technical schematics, and fragmentary communication logs. The most complete collection in Aurelia. Accessible only to Forum members.
2. **Physical Texts:** Books, printed on paper, preserved in Solara's council library. Approximately 200 volumes. Most are scientific or technical. One is a novel. The novel is the most read book in Aurelia.
3. **The Marsh Archive:** *P. memoriae* pools seeded with pre-Collapse latency — consciousness-residue from the era, absorbed by the bacteria. The patterns are fragmentary, centuries old, degraded. The Water Temple's oldest keeper has spent forty years attempting to read one pool. "It's a conversation," she reported. "Two people. They're arguing about something. I cannot tell what. The light is too faint."
