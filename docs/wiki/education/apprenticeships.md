# Traineeships & Apprenticeships — Aurelia

> *The federation has no universities. It has masters who teach, and learners who earn the right to learn.*

## The Apprenticeship Model

All Aurelian education beyond basic literacy is apprenticeship-based. A master — guild-certified, council-recognized, or community-acknowledged — takes on one to three apprentices at a time. The relationship is contractual in Solara and Valdris, informal in Mirithane, nonexistent in Arkos (where Vorns learn through data immersion) and The Verge (where learning is whatever someone teaches you).

Apprenticeship duration varies:
- **Solara research:** 5-7 years. Reef Labs apprentices study under a senior researcher. Cael-7 has trained three apprentices. One was Thren. One was human. One left for Mirithane before completing training.
- **Valdris guild:** Until the master deems the apprentice ready. No fixed duration. A mining apprentice may descend the deep shaft in year one or year five. The mountain determines readiness. The master interprets the mountain.
- **Mirithane keeper:** Five years exactly. The Water Temple's apprenticeship is the only formal curriculum in Mirithane. Years 1-3: bacterial pattern recognition. Year 4: supervised interpretation. Year 5: the test — a supplicant's question answered by reading the marsh's glow. If the interpretation brings tears, the apprentice is ordained.

## Type in Apprenticeship

**Humans:** Eligible for all apprenticeships. The default learner.

**Threns:** Eligible for all apprenticeships in theory. In practice, restricted in Solara — Thren apprentices may not train in fields deemed "sensitive" (council procedure, military planning, citizenship review administration). The restriction is not codified. The restriction is practice. Cael-7 was permitted to train in reef biology. He was not permitted to apply for council administrative apprenticeship. He did not apply. The not-applying is the ceiling.

**Vorns:** Eligible for Valdris guild apprenticeships (forge, mining, transport). Not eligible for Solara research apprenticeships — Solara's system is human-designed and assumes organic learning curves. A Vorn learns through data immersion, not mentorship. The incompatibility is functional, not discriminatory. The effect is the same.

**Glims:** Cannot be apprenticed. Glims do not learn in the sense apprenticeships require. The sanctuary keepers in Arkos observe that anomalous Glims exhibit learning-like behavior — improved pattern recognition over time, faster route adaptation. The observation is not apprenticeship. The observation is witness.

## The Loss of Knowledge

Every death of a master without a trained apprentice represents permanent knowledge loss. The Collapse destroyed the infrastructure of mass education. The apprenticeship model preserves knowledge but does not scale. The federation is losing skills: advanced metallurgy, pre-Collapse electronics repair, certain bacterial diagnostic techniques known to one elderly keeper with no apprentice yet named. The loss is accepted. The loss is the cost of the post-Collapse world.
