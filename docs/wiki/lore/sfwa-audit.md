## I. The World — Physical & Historical

| Question | Status | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|

## Priority Gap Ranking

### Tier 1 — World doesn't make sense without these
1. **Religion & Belief Systems** — Temples exist but are empty shells. What do people believe about sentience, death, the Collapse? This is the single biggest hole.
2. **The "Magic System"** — Memory extraction, bioluminescent storage, Glim emergence. The rules that make this world's central technology coherent.

### Tier 2 — World is coherent but flat without these
3. **Arts & Culture** — What do these people make? Sing? Celebrate beyond holidays?
4. **Daily Life narratives** — A day in the life for each type in each country.
5. **Language & Naming** — Conventions, dialects, the system behind the names.

### Tier 3 — Deep texture
6. **Crime & Legal System**
7. **Medicine per type**
8. **Fashion & Dress**
9. **Education**
10. **War & Military**
11. **Pre-Collapse civilization detail**
12. **Architecture per country**
13. **Detailed geography/maps**
